How to chage Firefox language

I am very sorry. There no a "language setting" in Firefox setting(ver 42.0).

If you want to change your Firefox language, there have some methon:

Uninstall Firefox but uncheck delete user data and intall a new Firefox with the language you want. :-)

or...(this methon is a litter hard)

1.goto here and install the language pack you want, just click Install Language Pack is OK.(if Firefox tell you "Not available for Firefox", goto Version Information on the buttom, chick "See complete version history" and choose your version to install.)

2.Type "about:config" to address bar in your Firefox. Change "general.useragent.locale" to your location.(such as:en-US,zh-TW)

3.Restart your Firefox.

btw, if you want to change the language of your add-ons, you can change "intl.locale.matchOS" to true.

I do this and successful change my Firefox language to English. If you have any question, email me, I will do my best to help you :-)
